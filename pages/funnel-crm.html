Example Scenario: Lead Nurture System

The Challenge

Imagine you're running a service-based business with Facebook ads that generate leads—but no system exists to nurture them. Inquiries come in, get added to a spreadsheet, and sit there. By the time your sales team follows up, most leads have gone cold and moved on to competitors.

Without automation: 3-5% conversion rate, inconsistent follow-up, wasted ad spend.

What I'd Build

An automated funnel with integrated CRM management:

  • Landing page automatically captures leads with minimal friction
  • Instant welcome email + SMS confirm they're heard
  • 7-day personalized nurture sequence based on which service they clicked
  • Engagement tracking flags hot leads and pushes them to the top of the call list
  • Non-responders get a re-engagement campaign after 2 weeks (win-back offers, new case studies)
  • All pipeline stages, notes, and history stored in one central CRM accessible to the team

Expected Impact

Systems like this typically see a 40-60% increase in lead-to-appointment rates by eliminating the follow-up gap and automating the nurture process.

Beyond conversion rates, you'd also see:

  • Faster sales cycles (days → hours)
  • Better team visibility into pipeline
  • Reduced manual data entry and follow-up friction
  • Higher email engagement due to timely, relevant messaging

What Gets Automated

Lead Capture & Tagging

Forms automatically sort leads by service interest, source, and engagement level. No manual data entry.

Email & SMS Sequences

Personalized multi-channel campaigns trigger based on actions. Send to 1 person or 1,000 instantly.

Pipeline Management

Leads move through stages automatically based on engagement. Sales team sees hot leads first.

Appointment Booking

Integrated calendars let leads self-schedule. Automatic reminders reduce no-shows.

Re-Engagement Campaigns

Inactive leads get targeted win-back sequences. Turn cold prospects warm without manual outreach.

Analytics & Reporting

Real-time dashboards show conversion rates, email metrics, pipeline health, and ROI by campaign.
