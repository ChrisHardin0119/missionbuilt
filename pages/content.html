Example Scenario: Tech Deals Content Engine

The Challenge

Imagine you're running a tech deals site and need consistent, engaging blog content published 3 times daily without writing a single word manually. You want to maintain brand voice, freshness, and SEO quality across hundreds of posts — but manual creation is impossible at that scale.

What I'd Build

A Claude API-powered content engine with custom prompt engineering. The system would maintain a consistent brand voice across every post, generate both long-form blog content and short social teasers from the same source data, and embed affiliate links naturally within the narrative.

How It Works

The process follows a simple pipeline:

  • Source Data: Product details, deal info, market context
  • Prompt Template: Custom instructions with brand voice guidelines
  • Claude API: Generates unique, conversational content
  • HTML Formatting: Automatic styling and structure
  • Auto-Publish: Direct to website or CMS

Expected Impact

Hundreds of unique blog posts generated, each maintaining the same conversational, deal-hunter personality. You'd get consistent content output without the writing burden, while readers experience a cohesive brand voice that keeps them coming back. Affiliate revenue increases thanks to natural link placement and higher volume of engaging content.
