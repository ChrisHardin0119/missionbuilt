How I'd Build This For You

Example Scenario: A Dental Practice

Imagine a local dental practice that's losing leads after hours. Patients call and get voicemail. Website visitors have questions about procedures or insurance but no way to get answers in real-time — so they bounce to a competitor. The practice has great reviews and solid services, but they're leaving money on the table every night and weekend.

What I'd Build

A 24/7 AI chatbot trained on their specific services, pricing, FAQs, insurance policies, and appointment availability. The bot would greet visitors, answer common questions, qualify whether someone is a good fit, and capture contact info for follow-up. It could also integrate with their booking system so patients can check availability and schedule directly — no phone call needed.

My Process

Here's how I'd take a project like this from idea to live deployment:

1

Discovery

Talk with you about your business, your customers, and the questions you get asked most. Map out what the bot needs to know.

2

Prompt Engineering

Build the bot's personality and knowledge base. Train it on your FAQs, services, pricing, and policies so it sounds like your brand.

3

Integration

Connect the bot to your booking system, CRM, or email platform — whatever tools you already use.

4

Testing

Run through real-world scenarios, edge cases, and make sure there's a smooth handoff to a human when the bot can't help.

5

Launch & Iterate

Deploy it on your site, monitor conversations, and refine the bot based on real user interactions.

Expected Impact

Chatbots like this typically capture 30-40% more leads by engaging visitors who would otherwise leave without reaching out. You'd get instant response times, after-hours coverage, and a system that gets smarter over time based on real conversations.
