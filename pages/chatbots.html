How I'd Build This For You

The Problem

A local dental practice was losing leads after hours. Potential patients would call and get voicemail. Website visitors browsing treatment options had questions with no way to get answers in real-time. They'd bounce to a competitor's site. The practice had solid reviews and good services, but the business was leaving money on the table.

The Solution

We built a 24/7 AI chatbot trained on their services, pricing, FAQ, insurance accepted, and appointment policies. The bot greets visitors, answers common questions, qualifies whether someone's actually a good fit for their practice, and captures contact information for follow-up. It's integrated with their booking system so visitors can check real availability and schedule directly.

The Build

Here's how we got from concept to deployment:

1

Requirements

Interview the practice owner. Map customer journeys. Identify top questions and pain points.

2

Prompt Engineering

Build the bot's "personality" and knowledge base. Train it on FAQs, service details, and insurance info.

3

Integration

Connect the bot to their booking API, CRM, and email system for lead capture and follow-up.

4

Testing

Real-world scenarios. Edge cases. Handoff to human agents when needed.

5

Deployment

Live on website. Monitor conversations. Iterate based on real user data.

The Result

A system like this typically captures 30-40% more leads by engaging visitors who would otherwise leave without even reaching out. The practice reduced after-hours lead loss, improved response time to zero seconds, and built a knowledge system they can continuously improve based on actual conversations.
